# Simone's garden 🌱

Time goes on and so we grow, like plants in a garden. Just as a tree has multiple branches, our lives are made up of many things pointing in different directions. For me, this blog is a place to explore those branches and to showcase who I am and what I like doing. 
As gardens can sometimes be quite messy, don't expect this blog to be always perfect and clear, although I will try my best to keep it clean :).
