## Why being bad at something is actually exciting

Excelling at everything is increasingly seen as a prerequisite for thriving in modern society. As a consequence of this a new trend is emerging: people are starting to focus on the things they are good at without leaving room for trying new things. Running the risk of being bad at something is not acceptable in a society where productivity is everything. To me, this is making people lose their playfulness; their willingness to explore new things without the need to be good at them right away.
We should rather embrace new things we are bad at. Being bad is actually exciting!
